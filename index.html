Our Story

A Place Where
Friends Become Family

At Folks & Roses, we believe that great food is best shared with great company. Our restaurant is designed to be your home away from home—a warm, inviting space where friends gather, stories are shared, and memories are made.

With an array of cuisines from around the world, we take you on a culinary journey. From hearty breakfasts to elegant dinners, every dish is crafted with passion and the finest ingredients.

Meet Our Chief Chef JB

With over 10 years of culinary excellence, our head chef has served distinguished national and international guests, bringing world-class dining experience to Kampala. From state banquets to private celebrations, every dish reflects expertise, passion, and attention to detail.
